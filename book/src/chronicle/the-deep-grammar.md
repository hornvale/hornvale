# The Deep Grammar

*The worldview stops being what the sentence says, and becomes how the
word is built.*

Through six campaigns the chorus diverged in content: what a culture
holds, why it thinks so, who teaches it. This campaign — the metaplan's
seventh — pushes the divergence into morphology. Tongues now carry
evidential marking (*how does the speaker know?*) and noun-class marking
(*what kind of thing is it?*), and the depth of that marking — nothing,
a free particle, a bound affix — is drawn per species from
typology-weighted streams. Most tongues mark nothing, as most human
languages do; the deep ones are the marked minority, and in them the
Book's oldest lines changed shape:

> Saa **Wo**we Vavako. ("The Vavako are goblins.")
> Saa **Wo**vewe Vebe. ("Vebe is the earth.")

The goblin tongue drew a noun-class affix, so every marked noun now
carries its class prefix — drawn from goblin phonology, attached at the
segment level so the romanization treats the joined word as one word,
never two glued strings. The hobgoblin tongue drew particles instead,
and its evidential particle is where the campaign's payoff lands. The
folk say:

> Vebe Vebe **Be** Bo. ("Vebe is the earth.")

and their priesthood, teaching the same proposition, says:

> Vebe Vebe **Bo** Bo. ("Vebe is the earth — as it is taught.")

One particle differs: *Be*, the witnessed evidential, against *Bo*, the
taught one. The evidential's value is never a fresh judgment — it is the
shipped epistemic machinery read out. A statement grounded in a
culture's own steeped experience is *witnessed* (the lexicon's exposure
class — the knowledge-to-evidentiality mapping the theory promised,
executed on state that has existed since the lexicon campaign); an institutionally
transmitted line is *taught* (the sixth campaign's doctrine speaking
in-tongue — the reportative used in instruction, as Quechua uses it);
an inference-grounded statement would be *inferred*, a value the enum
defines, the floor cannot reach, and a loud guard protects.

The morpheme forms themselves are the campaign's quiet depth. Each
marker's proto-form is drawn once per language *family* and evolved into
every daughter through the same exceptionless sound-change cascade the
roots ride — so goblin and hobgoblin carry *cognate* morphology with
regular sound correspondences (seed 3's marker pairs — Zqoo/Zqoozh,
Seo/Sheosh — differ exactly where their cascades differ). Grammatical
substance is conservative and family-shaped; whether a species
grammaticalizes at all is fast and species-drawn. Both halves are real
typology, and neither required authoring a single surface form.

And the noun classes close a loop the fifth campaign opened. Class
assignment is derived, never drawn: people-kinds are animate, the world's
furniture inanimate — *unless the culture's own drawn cosmology says
otherwise*. A culture whose day-explanation drew the agentive schema
grammatically animates the sky: the sun and moons take the animate class
because, for that culture, they are somebody. The noun-class system and
the folk cosmology are one worldview fact read at two layers, and a
standing law asserts the two layers can never disagree. At the measured
seeds, one culture animates its sky — a hobgoblin people whose priests
already taught that a god strides it.

The close absorbed a lively main: a parallel campaign's settlement
re-baseline moved a kobold people into seed 3's world, and the suite's
depth landscape re-pinned honestly — the new tongue drew its own class
affix, and the same world's hobgoblin cult went folk under the new
demography, dropping its priesthood section exactly as the institution
gate demands. The machinery flexed around a moving world without a
single law bending, which is the whole program's proposition in
miniature.

One honest blemish is recorded rather than hidden: at the flagship seed,
the goblin animate and inanimate markers drew the same syllable — the
class system there marks *presence* without distinguishing class. Real
languages syncretize their gender marking constantly, so the ledger
recommends leaving it; whether to make the marker draws collision-aware
(as the root lexicon's are) is a fidelity question presented, not
decided.

What waits: verbs — voice and valency, the causal filter's own
morphology, when tongues learn to say more than what things are; and the
diachronic Book, where this campaign's conservative morphemes and fast
grammaticalization finally get their time axis.
