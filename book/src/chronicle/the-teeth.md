# The Teeth

The Quarry read a field of predators and taught the prey to flee it. The Wilding
gave the prey eyes to feel that fear as agents. Both were the *eaten*'s half of the
oldest interaction in biology. This campaign builds the *eater*'s half — not the
kill, not yet, but the hunger that leads to it: a carnivore *drawn* to where its
prey is, exactly as a herbivore is repelled from where its predators are.

The symmetry was the whole design, and it had been waiting since The Dread first
wrote `threat_value = threat_niche · hazard_field`. That formula has a twin:
`food_value = diet_niche · resource_field`, the same shape with the sign turned
over — approach where the one is avoidance. The Quarry built the predator field so
a quarry could flee *up its gradient reversed*; The Teeth builds the field's
mirror, a **prey** field, so a carnivore's hunger can climb *toward* it. The two
fields are duals over the very same coexistence stack: where the predator field
sums the realized density of the carnivores, the prey field sums the density of the
prey base — the herbivores and grazers a hunter eats. One stack, two populations,
two signs. The eater and the eaten, reading the same land in opposite directions.

And so predation-approach turned out not to be a new drive at all. It is the
`ANIMAL_PREY` axis of the hunger The Provender already built — the axis that, until
now, quietly grazed plant productivity for its meat, as though a wolf could eat a
meadow. The Teeth routes that axis to the prey field instead: a carnivore's food is
other creatures, so its hunger is drawn to where the creatures are. The same
authored number that reads *forward* as a diet's taste for flesh now reads its own
field, the standing prey of a place.

The change is, like The Quarry's, a sleeping one. The prey term is *additive* — it
only ever raises a cell's worth as food — so a creature that already eats where it
stands goes on eating where it stands, untouched. The settled peoples of every
world, sat by The Confluence on productive ground, never forage and so never feel
the pull; seed 42's possession walk is byte-for-byte what it was, the wild
rust-monster in its midst none the wiser. The hunger bites only for a creature that
must *forage* on prey-poor ground — a wild carnivore beast on the barren edges,
which now, instead of wandering the productivity gradient blind, turns and heads for
the herds. On uniform ground with prey concentrated in one direction, a carnivore
goes that way and a herbivore, deaf to the prey field, does not. The hunt has a
heading.

But a heading is not a kill. The carnivore is drawn toward its prey and stops there;
it does not yet close the distance, does not target the particular elk in the herd,
does not catch it, does not eat it, does not leave it dead. That — the visible
pursuit, the specific quarry, the death that feeds one creature by ending another —
is the anti-symmetric heart of predation, the place where the two signs stop being a
tidy mirror and one of them *dies*. It waits in the reserved tier, with the slow
tides of predator and prey rising and falling against each other over the years.
The Quarry gave the wild its fear; The Wilding gave it eyes; The Teeth gives it
hunger and a direction. The bite itself is still to come.
