# The Book

## Volume 1: Vebe

Vebe is a planet orbiting a yellow-white dwarf (F), with two moons; its day lasts about 1.5 standard days.

## Volume 2: Waobwoe

Waobwoe is a planet orbiting a yellow-white dwarf (F), with one moon; its day lasts about 1.2 standard days.

## Volume 3: Zhqea

Zhqea is a planet orbiting an orange dwarf (K), with one moon; its day lasts about 0.7 standard days.
