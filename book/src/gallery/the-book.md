# The Book

## Volume 1: Vebe

Vebe is a planet with two moons, orbiting a yellow-white dwarf (F); its day lasts about 1.5 standard days.
The Vavako are goblins.
The Babako are hobgoblins.

## Volume 2: Waobwoe

Waobwoe is a planet with one moon, orbiting a yellow-white dwarf (F); its day lasts about 1.2 standard days.
The Maetmea are goblins.
The Waedwea are hobgoblins.
The Ngkoshngta are kobolds.

## Volume 3: Zhqea

Zhqea is a planet with one moon, orbiting an orange dwarf (K); its day lasts about 0.7 standard days.
The Sdeozqae are goblins.
The Shteozqae are hobgoblins.
